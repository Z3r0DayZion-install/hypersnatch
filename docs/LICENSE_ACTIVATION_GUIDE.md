# HyperSnatch — License Activation Guide

Thank you for purchasing HyperSnatch! Follow these steps to activate your license.

---

## Step 1: Get Your Hardware ID (HWID)

1. Open HyperSnatch
2. Look at the header bar — you'll see a green chip icon with a code like `7bbe0d87d0028d23`
3. **Click it** to copy your full Hardware ID to your clipboard
4. It will say "Hardware Node ID copied to clipboard"

> Your HWID is a SHA-256 hash unique to your machine. It never leaves your computer.

---

## Step 2: Send Your HWID

Reply to your Gumroad purchase receipt email with:

- Your **full HWID** (64 characters, starts with something like `7bbe0d87...`)
- The **email** you used to purchase

We'll generate your license key within 12 hours.

---

## Step 3: Import Your License

1. You'll receive a `license.json` file via email
2. Open HyperSnatch
3. Click the **🔒 Import Key** button in the header bar
4. Select your `license.json` file
5. Your tier badge will update to **SOVEREIGN EDITION** (or INSTITUTIONAL)

That's it — your license is now hardware-bound and works fully offline. No account needed, no internet required.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| "Hardware ID Mismatch" | You're using a license generated for a different machine. Send us your new HWID. |
| "License Expired" | Your 1-year license has expired. Contact us for renewal. |
| "Signature Invalid" | The license file may be corrupted. Request a fresh copy. |
| Badge still shows "COMMUNITY" | Make sure you selected the correct `license.json` file. |
