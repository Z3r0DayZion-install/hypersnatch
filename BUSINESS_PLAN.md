# HyperSnatch Business Plan

## Executive Summary
HyperSnatch is an offline-first binary verification system that enables secure software distribution without network dependencies. Our technology solves critical security and privacy challenges in software supply chains, targeting a $2B+ market in binary verification and DevOps security.
