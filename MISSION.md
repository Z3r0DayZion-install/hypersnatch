# HyperSnatch: The Sovereign Evidence Standard
## IPO Mission & Vision Statement

### **The Problem**
Digital evidence is increasingly obfuscated, volatile, and distributed across fragmented platforms. Current forensic suites are either "Too Big to Fail" (expensive, slow, non-portable) or "Too Small to Trust" (script-based, non-deterministic, no chain of custody).

### **The HyperSnatch Solution**
HyperSnatch is the world's first **Deterministic Offline Evidence Resurrection Platform**. We provide a sub-second, zero-trust environment for extracting and validating digital artifacts from seized assets without network exposure.

### **IPO-Grade Value Drivers (The "Gold")**
1.  **TEAR Protocol (v3.1.0)**: A proprietary, deterministic state-machine for artifact resurrection that ensures 100% repeatability in court.
2.  **Sovereign Trust Architecture**: Moving away from centralized CAs to a decentralized, hardware-bound trust model (CPU/MAC binding).
3.  **Airgap Supremacy**: Optimized for restricted environments where data exfiltration is a felony risk.
4.  **Extensible Intelligence**: A signed ecosystem of "Strategy Packs" that allows the platform to adapt to new obfuscation techniques in real-time.

### **The Target Market**
- **Public Sector**: State/Federal Law Enforcement & Intelligence.
- **Private Sector**: Fortune 500 Incident Response (IR) teams and Forensic Consultants.
- **Defense**: Cyber-warfare units requiring portable triage tools.
