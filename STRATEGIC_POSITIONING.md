# HyperSnatch - Strategic Positioning & Capital Narrative

## Executive Summary

HyperSnatch is a structured forensic endpoint reconstruction platform designed for the modern era of digital artifact complexity. Unlike general-purpose scraping tools, we provide a policy-forward, offline-first solution for reconstructing buried digital artifacts from static HTML snapshots and HAR captures.
